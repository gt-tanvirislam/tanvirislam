Process

How I Work

A transparent, streamlined process that ensures every project is delivered with precision and care.

01

Discovery & Discussion

We start with a detailed conversation about your goals, brand, target audience, and vision. I ask the right questions to fully understand what you need before touching any tool.

02

Creative Planning

I develop a clear creative brief and action plan — including style direction, timeline, and deliverables. You'll know exactly what to expect before work begins.

03

Editing & Designing

This is where the magic happens. Using industry-leading tools, I craft your project with meticulous attention to every detail — color, timing, composition, and impact.

04

Review & Revision

You review the first draft and provide feedback. I offer free revisions to ensure the final result perfectly aligns with your vision. Your satisfaction is my priority.

05

Final Delivery

The polished, final files are delivered in your preferred format on or before the deadline. I also provide ongoing support after delivery if needed.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to complete a video editing project?
+
Delivery time depends on the project's complexity. A standard YouTube video (5-15 min) typically takes 2-4 days. Short reels are delivered in 24-48 hours. I always discuss timelines upfront and meet deadlines consistently.
How many revisions do I get?
+
Every package includes free revisions (usually 2-3, more on premium packages). I want you to be 100% satisfied with the result. Additional revisions beyond the package limit are available at a small fee.
What file formats do you deliver in?
+
For video: MP4 (H.264/H.265), MOV, and other formats on request. For graphic design: PNG, JPG, PDF, SVG, and editable source files (AI, PSD). Just let me know your requirements.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely! I work with clients from the USA, UK, Europe, Australia, and across Asia. Communication is through email, WhatsApp, or any platform you prefer. Payment via international methods available.
