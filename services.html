FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with me. Still have questions? Ask me directly →

Video Editing

What video software do you use?
+
Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, DaVinci Resolve for color grading, and After Effects for motion graphics. All industry-standard tools ensuring the highest quality output for every project.
How long does video editing take?
+
Short reels (30–60 sec): 24–48 hours. YouTube videos (5–15 min): 3–5 days. Long-form (30+ min): 5–10 days. Rush delivery is available for an extra fee. I always confirm timelines upfront.
What raw footage formats do you accept?
+
MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV — from smartphones, DSLRs, GoPros, and drones. If your footage is in an unusual format, just ask. I can usually work with it or convert it without extra charge.
Can you optimise videos for specific platforms?
+
Yes — 9:16 vertical for TikTok/Reels, 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for Instagram feed, 4:5 for ads. I understand each platform's algorithm and edit accordingly for maximum reach.
Do you add subtitles or captions?
+
Yes! Styled captions, lower thirds, and SRT files for YouTube are all available. Subtitles are properly synced and look great — included in certain packages or available as an add-on.

Pricing & Payment

How do I pay for your services?
+
PayPal, Wise, bKash, and bank transfer accepted. New clients: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Returning clients and long-term projects get flexible payment terms.
Are there discounts for bulk orders?
+
Yes! 5+ videos or 10+ designs: 10–20% discount. Monthly retainer packages offer the best rates and are ideal for businesses needing regular content production.
Are there any hidden fees?
+
Never. I quote clearly upfront and you only pay what's agreed. Any extras (stock footage, licensed music, etc.) are always communicated and approved before being added.

Graphic Design

What design software do you use?
+
Adobe Photoshop for raster graphics, Illustrator for vector artwork and logos, Canva Pro for social templates, and Figma for UI mockups. Delivered in print-ready and web-ready formats.
Will I receive the source files?
+
Yes! All packages include source files (PSD, AI, etc.) on request. I keep backups for 6 months after project completion so you can request files again at any time.
Can you match my existing brand guidelines?
+
Absolutely. Share your brand guidelines, color codes, fonts, and existing assets — I'll ensure all new designs are perfectly on-brand and consistent with your established identity.
What file formats do you deliver?
+
Design: PNG, JPG, PDF, SVG, AI, PSD. Video: MP4 (H.264/H.265), MOV. Additional formats available on request — just specify your requirements when placing the order.

Delivery & Revisions

How many revisions do I get?
+
Basic: 2 revisions. Standard: 3. Premium: unlimited. One revision = one round of consolidated feedback. Detailed feedback upfront means fewer rounds needed.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result?
+
Your satisfaction is my top priority. I revise until you're happy. If after all revisions you're still not satisfied, I'll refund your payment. This has never happened — I work hard to get it right!
Do you work with international clients?
+
Absolutely! I work with clients from the USA, UK, Europe, Australia, and across Asia. Flexible communication via email, WhatsApp, and Zoom. Time zones are never a barrier.
Can I request rush delivery?
+
Yes — rush delivery (24–48 hr) adds a 30–50% fee to the base price. Contact me early for rush projects to ensure availability. I always prioritize quality even on tight deadlines.
